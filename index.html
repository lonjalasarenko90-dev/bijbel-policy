✝ Privacy Policy

Біблія Благодаті — Bible of Grace

Short version: This app does not collect, share, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Біблія Благодаті does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any servers.

2. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only, using the device's local storage (localStorage / SharedPreferences):

  • Bookmarks you create
  • Verse highlights
  • Personal notes and comments
  • Reading position (last-read chapter)
  • App settings (font size, line height)
  • Scripture source attribution (if entered)

This data is never uploaded, shared, or accessible to the developer or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media integrations
  • Any network connections

4. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

The app uses the device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine (Android TTS / Web Speech API) to read Scripture aloud. This feature is provided entirely by the operating system and does not involve any data transmission to the app developer. Some TTS engines may connect to the internet for voice data — this is controlled entirely by your device settings.

5. Export Feature

The app allows you to export your bookmarks and notes to a JSON file saved on your device. This file remains on your device; the app does not upload it anywhere.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is suitable for all ages.

7. Scripture Text

The biblical text used in this app is either placeholder content or text from public-domain or appropriately licensed sources, as indicated within the app's Settings → Scripture Source section.
